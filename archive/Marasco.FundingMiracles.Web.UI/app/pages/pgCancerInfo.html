Funding Miracles for Cancer Cases

Cancer has impacted people in one way or another. Whether it be a close friend or relative or the neighbor down the street, more and more people are being diagnosed with this condition every day. As a result, online fundraising for cancer has increased within the past few years. As the costs associated to treating cancer can be a financial burden for many, Funding Miracles was created to allow for those in need of financial help to get the most amount available through online funding. To understand more about cancer, please read below.

What is Cancer?

Cancer is a general term for a group of diseases caused by uncontrollable cell growth. As cells are divide, tumors are form which can invade parts of the body. Although there have been many studies on risks of cancer, the reality is the cause of cancer is not clearly understood. Research has linked many substances to cancer such as diet, exercise, environment, chemicals, genetics, etc.
