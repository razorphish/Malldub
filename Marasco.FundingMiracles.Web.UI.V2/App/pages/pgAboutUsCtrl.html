Funding Miracles is one of the leading low cost crowdfunding websites in the world. Anyone can create a fundraiser and with Funding Miracle’s easy fundraising campaign creator, users will be able to create and publish fundraisers within minutes. You Create. They Donate. We Change Lives.

FREE Online Fundraising Websites For Medical Expenses

We have devoted our lives to providing a fundraising service that people could use to help other people in need at no cost. The only fees charged are the ones that are out of our control. However, we remain focused on giving you the best possible fundraising service available to make up those costs.
