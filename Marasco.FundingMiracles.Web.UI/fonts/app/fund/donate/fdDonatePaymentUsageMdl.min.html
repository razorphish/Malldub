How Miracles Uses Payments

Funding Miracles never charges. We operate mainly by supporters and their generous donations. Unlike other fundraising sites who charge 5-10% of the donations raised, Funding Miracles charges $0 in website fees. An optional donation to Miracles is available after every donation transaction to help operations costs and keep our lights on. We want to thank the thousands of Miracle donors who have helped keep this free of costs for fundraisers to use.
